Kebab

Ingredients

  • 500g boneless chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1/4 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Lemon wedges, for serving
  • Fresh parsley or mint, for garnish

Recipe

  1. In a bowl, mix yogurt, olive oil, minced garlic, and all the spices.
  2. Add the chicken pieces to the marinade, ensuring they are well-coated. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour.
  3. Preheat the grill or oven to medium-high heat.
  4. Thread the marinated chicken pieces onto skewers.
  5. Grill the chicken kebabs for about 6-8 minutes on each side or until cooked through.
  6. Remove from heat and let them rest for a few minutes.
  7. Serve the Arabian chicken kebabs hot, garnished with lemon wedges and fresh parsley or mint.

Enjoy!
